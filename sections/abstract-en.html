Abstract

This ontology, titled Smart Tourism Recommendation System, has been developed as part of the PEC 2 for the subject “Representación del Conocimiento” at the Universitat Oberta de Catalunya (UOC). It models a semantic structure for the tourism domain, representing entities such as cities, events, restaurants, hotels, attractions, and transport services. The ontology supports reasoning and recommendation based on context-aware queries to enhance personalized travel planning.
